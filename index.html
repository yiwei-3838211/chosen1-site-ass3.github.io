Project Description

Overview

Topic

Online education refers to the method of content dissemination and rapid learning through the application of information technology and internet technology. In recent years, the development of hardware devices, online real-time transmission technology has driven the rapid development of online education. At present, the technology of online education is mainly based on interactive platforms and intelligent systems. And with the continuous development of technology, more and more technologies are applied to online education platforms to optimize the teaching experience and effectiveness of the platforms and promote the upgrading of products and services in the online education industry.

Compared to traditional offline education methods, online education can break the constraints of time and space, meet the needs of consumers to obtain education in fragmented time, and make up for the shortcomings of traditional education learning time and place inflexibility. The application of artificial intelligence, big data and other technologies in the internet education industry can enable educators to grasp the learning situation of users in real time and accurately. Under the premise of resource sharing, users have a high degree of freedom in choosing education brands and teaching styles.

Motivation

In the first half of 2020, influenced by the COVID-19, the penetration rate of the online education industry increased significantly. Over 1.2 billion youngsters are out of school worldwide. The epidemic has brought forward the overall process of the online education industry by three to five years. The online education industry has seen a significant increase in penetration, opening the valve for users to shift from offline to online and change their behavioural habits. Meanwhile, the penetration of big data and AI technologies in education will continue to deepen. Online learning industry became the focus of capital development, and the major Internet giants also accelerated their entry into the game.

Landscape

Recently, there are many established online education platforms which are already in the market and widely used, such as Udemy, Coursera and Skillshare. However, the challenges also exist for online institutions in promoting online learning and teaching. Firstly, the pressure on the server multiplied during the epidemic, and the live streaming lag, delay and crash phenomenon affect the teaching effect. Secondly, poor interactivity in online classes and lack of feedback and analysis systems make it difficult to guarantee teaching quality. For our new online learning platforms, we will devote ourselves to creating optimization systems in order to deal with basic technical issues such as delayed transmission and video lag.

Detailed Description

Aims

The purpose of this project is to create a platform that allows students to take classes anywhere. The aim is to enable some students who are far away from the campus due to unavoidable factors to obtain the same teaching quality.

This project has the following objectives

A) Accessibility Of Time And Place

The advantage of online education is that it allows students to take classes from any location they choose. This software needs to allow students to use this software for classes at home away from school. And also It can allow schools to reach a wider network of students without being restricted by geographic boundaries.

B) High Efficiency

Online learning provides teachers with an effective way to teach students. There are many tools for this software that need to be included, such as videos, podcasts, and PDFs. Teachers can use these tools for part of their lesson plans. This can enable teachers to become more efficient educators if they extend their lesson plans beyond traditional textbooks to include online resources.

C) Inexpensive

Another important advantage of online learning is that it can reduce costs. Because Online learning needs to eliminate student transportation, student meals, and most importantly real estate costs. And all courses or learning materials need to be available online, it can create a paperless learning environment that is more economical and beneficial to the environment.

D) Can Suits A Variety Of Learning Styles

Every student has a different learning process and learning style. Some students are visual learners, and others prefer to learn through audio. This software will provide a variety of options and resources, which allows each student to choose the learning styles they need.

Scope and Limits

In this project, According to the plan, when it is completed it will be an online education platform. This software will be able to run on windows, ios and android. When it is delivered, the functions that it should have include online video, online chat and online screen sharing. For the tools in the software, such as drawing and emoticons, we will use things that already exist. This software can be used without creating a personal account. Setting up a server is not planned in this project.

Risks

The risks included in this project will be the following

A) The network latency is too high during a video call

Because this software will be used all over the world, the distance between lecturers and students may be too far. Such as Australia and the United States. This may result in students not being able to interact with the lecturer, even unable to see the lecturer’s sharing screen. The possible solution to this problem is to build more servers in the future

B) Time zone differences

Sometimes, it can be difficult to have a video conference with people in the country or other parts of the world just because of the time zone. For example, the 3:00 pm meeting in the United States will be in the middle of the night in England. A possible solution to this problem is to add meeting recording tools

C) The software is too complicated to use

In this project, this software may be too complicated for users. This causes users to spend a lot of time learning how to use it. The possible solution to this problem is to make the software design as simple and easy to use as possible.
